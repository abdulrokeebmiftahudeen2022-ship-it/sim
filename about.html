Stay In Motion Travel and Tours is a premium travel agency based in Ibadan, Nigeria. We specialize in making travel seamless from local flights within Nigeria to international journeys across the globe.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Truth

Stay In Motion was born from one observation: Nigerians love to travel, but the process can be stressful. Booking flights, securing visas, finding reliable packages it often feels like a maze.

We started in Ibadan with a simple mission: make travel effortless. No more endless searches, no more uncertainty. Just honest advice, fast support, and journeys that actually happen without the headache.

Today, we've helped thousands of travelers explore 45+ countries, and we're just getting started.
