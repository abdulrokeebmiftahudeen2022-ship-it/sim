Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when booking our services, including: name, email address, phone number, passport details, travel preferences, and payment information. We also collect data through website usage (cookies, IP addresses).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: process bookings, provide visa assistance, communicate about your travel plans, improve our services, send promotional offers (with your consent), and comply with legal requirements.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only as necessary: with airlines, hotels, and other travel providers to fulfill your booking; with embassies for visa processing; and when required by law. We never sell your personal information.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, request corrections, request deletion, opt out of marketing communications, and withdraw consent. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at stayinmotionenterprise@gmail.com or +234 805 365 2978.
