Cancellation Policy

Understanding our cancellation and refund process.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Flight Bookings

Cancellation policies vary by airline. Most airlines allow cancellation with fees. We will inform you of the specific policy before booking. Refunds are processed according to airline timelines (typically 7-21 business days).

2. Travel Packages

Cancellation fees apply based on notice period:

  • 30+ days before travel: 80% refund
  • 15-29 days before travel: 50% refund
  • 7-14 days before travel: 25% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

3. Visa Services

Visa assistance fees are non-refundable once the application has been submitted to the embassy. Embassy visa fees are non-refundable regardless of outcome.

4. How to Cancel

To cancel a booking, contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +234 802 664 1531 or email at stayinmotionenterprise@gmail.com. Cancellation is effective on the date we receive your request.

5. Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 7-14 business days via the original payment method. Bank transfers may take additional time.

6. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen cancellations, medical emergencies, and trip interruptions.

7. Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, political instability, or other unforeseen events beyond our control, cancellation policies may be adjusted. We will work with you to find the best possible solution.

8. Contact Us

For questions about cancellations, contact us at stayinmotionenterprise@gmail.com or +234 805 365 2978.
